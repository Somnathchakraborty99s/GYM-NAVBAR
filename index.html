    about this company

    SPCL Infotech Services Private Limited, founded in May 2018 and headquartered on Sinhgad Road, Pune, is an active, unlisted IT and business services firm with minimal share capital (~₹1 lakh). It specializes in software development, digital marketing, analytics, ERP, and enterprise training. Pritee Prakash Wagh and Sanjay Shankarlal Shah serve as current directors. As of mid‑2022, its latest AGM and financial filings were timely. Employee feedback rates the company positively for job stability and workplace environment, though pay and trainee fees have received some criticism.
